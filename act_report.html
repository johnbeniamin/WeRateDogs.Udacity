Retweets vs favourites¶

In [645]:
plt.subplots(1, sharex=True)
tweets.retweet_count.groupby([tweets["timestamp"].dt.year, tweets["timestamp"].dt.month]).mean().plot(kind='line')
tweets.favorite_count.groupby([tweets["timestamp"].dt.year, tweets["timestamp"].dt.month]).mean().plot(kind='line').set_ylim(0, )
plt.title('Retweeting and Favoriting trend over time')
plt.ylabel('Count')
plt.xlabel('(Year, Month)')
plt.legend(('Retweets', 'Favorites'))
plt.savefig('ret_fav')

by the time and noticing the relation between retweets and favourites, you will find that favourites is increasingly dramatically more than retweets, and the gap is contiously incresing, in 2016 for example the gap was about 3000 but iin 2017 the gap was about 30000, that may have a lot of meaning like decreasing in the social sense, or may be an increasing usage of favourite as a way to save tweets to return for sometime later¶
